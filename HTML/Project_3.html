About company

ECOVACS Group was founded in 1998 by entrepreneur Chen Dongchi, who became its president. The main activity of the ECOVACS Group is the production of electronics. In 2006, the ECOVACS ROBOTICS division was created - a company that specializes in the development, production and sale of household robotics. ECOVACS ROBOTICS, whose slogan is “Use smart technology. Enjoy Life ”, produces innovative products to help people with their daily household chores, improving living standards and comfort. The company's product line includes four categories of household robotics: DEEBOT floor cleaning devices, WINBOT window cleaning devices, ATMOBOT portable air purification and humidification devices, and FAMIBOT devices for entertainment and safety.

ECOVACS ROBOTICS is one of the top three home robotics companies around the world. ECOVACS ROBOTICS is the most popular brand in China with a 65% market share in the largest and fastest growing economy in the world. The company currently has more than 600 stores and a well-developed sales network, distributing products to most of the large and medium-sized cities in mainland China. The company has over 5,000 employees worldwide; central offices are located in Canton (Ohio, USA), Dusseldorf (Germany) and Suzhou (China). Products are successfully sold on 6 continents in more than 31 countries, including the United States, Canada, Switzerland, France, Japan, Czech Republic, Poland, Malaysia and Australia.
